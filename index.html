About Me

I am Kothapally Sai Charan Teja, a passionate Level Designer and Unity Developer currently pursuing my 3rd-year in Computer Science Engineering at SR University.

My primary interest lies in designing engaging and interactive game experiences. I focus on building smooth and responsive gameplay systems using Unity, with a strong understanding of physics, collision handling, and player mechanics.

I have hands-on experience developing multiple projects such as JetClutch, an Angry Birds Clone, and 2D Platformer games. Through these, I explored concepts like projectile motion, level balancing, player movement systems, and game optimization.

I enjoy solving complex gameplay problems and turning ideas into playable experiences. My approach focuses on user experience, smooth controls, and creating levels that feel natural and engaging for players.

Along with Unity and C#, I continuously improve my skills in game design principles, level layout planning, and performance optimization to build high-quality games.

My goal is to become a professional Game Developer and Level Designer, contributing to innovative gaming projects and building experiences that players truly enjoy.
